A Gaol Breathes Again

A publicly owned redundant gaol, dating from around the start of the 18th Century. The Parish Council appointed me to advise them on the replacement of the sand:cement render with a lime based render. Working closely with the Local Authority Conservation Officer, I attained a Listed Building Consent approval for the works. Project reference code 108.
