BUILDING SURVEYS

We pride ourselves on our personal touch, pragmatic approach and attention to detail to suit your project needs.

We can advise on all building types and uses; residential, commercial, ecclesiastical, agricultural or industrial heritage and at all stages of your property’s life cycle.

While we are specialists in work to listed buildings, we also advise on older unlisted buildings, that either require an understanding of historic construction methods or an approach that is sensitive to their historic character.

We also provide building condition surveys and, specialising in historic property, means that we are best placed to advise you on the most appropriate repairs.

Where works require planning permission and/or listed building consent, we can provide advice and prepare and submit applications on your behalf.

So whether it’s an alteration or extension project or advice on a building’s condition, repair or conservation, contact us for an initial consultation meeting.

Peter Hayes’ professional background is as a Chartered Building Surveyor. Peter has a wide spectrum of experience within the historic built environment and has worked for both the National Trust and in private practice.

To compliment his practical experience Peter achieved a Distinction in his Masters Degree in Conservation of the Historic Environment and is currently working towards the RICS Building Conservation Accreditation.

Peter is also an affiliate member of the Institute of Historic Building Conservation (IHBC), a member of the Buildings Limes Forum and the Society for the Protection of Ancient Buildings (SPAB).
